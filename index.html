Key Features

Processor: Intel i5-6600k

The CPU that is powering my rig is the unlocked Intel 6th Gen. overclockable i5-6600k processor.
The base clock speed is 3.5Ghz, however, I have my CPU overclocked to 4.6Ghz.


Motherboard: MSI Z170A Gaming M5

Some noteworthy features of my motherboard are:

  • Onboard CPU Temperatures (in Celsius)
  • SLI & CrossFire Support
  • Raid support
  • DDR4 RAM configurable up to 64GB at 3600Mhz

Graphics Card: EVGA GTX 960

The graphics card that I currently have is an EVGA FTW GTX 960 with 2GB of GDDR5 memory.
I mainly game in 1080p at 60 frames, but I recently bought a 4k TV and I am looking to experiment with 4K gaming.
The GPU I have now is not capable of playing 2160p at 120fps so I will be looking to upgrade the card the preferably a MSI GTX 1070 or 1080.
